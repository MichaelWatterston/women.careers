About Women.Careers

Women.Careers is a dedicated platform empowering women with career development tools, mentorship, and access to curated job programs. Whether you're changing careers, returning to the workforce, or climbing the leadership ladder, we’re here to support your journey.

Featured Opportunities

Tech Fellowship

A 12-week virtual program for women starting or switching into tech. Includes mentorship and hands-on projects.

Leadership Bootcamp

A 6-month course designed for women looking to level up in management and executive roles.

Resume & Interview Coaching

One-on-one coaching sessions to prepare you for job applications, interviews, and salary negotiation.
